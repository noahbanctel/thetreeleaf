about us

We are a collective launched in 2023 by Noah Banctel, then a student at the Iéseg School of Management. The idea is to bring together individuals in areas affected
by human activity, and to act discreetly without the slightest incident, in order to help the development of wildlife and flora.
Today, with over a hundred members, we're striving to grow and raise awareness among local and global populations alike. To do this, we raise funds to enable the leaves to regain those colors of yesteryear in the places where we operate.

Our missions and commitmentsin relation to the environment

Our mission is to improve the environment so that future generations can walk in a healthy, green world. We want the planet to thrive, and to become as green as it once was.

We are aware of the stakes involved, and want to implement a long-term, sustainable action plan so that future generations can take over what we have already done.

Our visionsHow do we see the planet evolving through our actions?

Our long-term vision is to enable people all over the world to live in harmony with a healthy ecosystem and respect for the resources the planet provides.

We see planet Earth as a house whose foundations are crumbling, so we need to strengthen them.

Our actions for the planet How do we keep the planet going?

We are particularly active in the French Landes and the Black Forest in Germany, replanting trees and developing endangered flora through controlled regrowth schemes.

In this way, we promote the development of wild fauna, which evolves in protected and safeguarded habitats, without any danger to man. On the contrary, we need them to ensure stable biodiversity.
